🏔️ Mount Everest

The Highest Mountain in the World

🏔️ About Mount Everest

Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world. It stands at 8,849 meters (29,032 feet) above sea level. It is located in the Himalayas on the border of Nepal and China. Every year, thousands of climbers visit Mount Everest to experience its beauty and challenge.

📜 History

Mount Everest was first successfully climbed on 29 May 1953 by Sir Edmund Hillary of New Zealand and Tenzing Norgay Sherpa of Nepal. Since then, it has become one of the world's most famous climbing destinations.

⭐ Why Famous

• Highest mountain on Earth.
• Popular destination for mountain climbers.
• Located in the beautiful Himalayan range.
• Symbol of courage and adventure.

🏗️ Features

• Height: 8,849 meters
• Snow-covered peak
• Rocky mountains
• Glaciers and icefalls
• Extreme weather conditions

✨ Interesting Facts

  • Mount Everest is the tallest mountain on Earth.
  • It lies between Nepal and China.
  • More than 6,000 people have reached its summit.
  • Its local Nepali name is Sagarmatha.
  • Its Tibetan name is Chomolungma.

  • Mountain Range: Himalayas
