🤍 Taj Mahal

The Symbol of Love and India's Greatest Monument

🏛️ About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful and famous monuments in the world.
It is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, beside the Yamuna River.
It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.
The construction started in 1632 AD and was completed around 1653 AD.
Thousands of skilled workers, architects, and artists worked together to create this amazing monument using white marble and precious stones.
The Taj Mahal represents eternal love, beauty, and dedication.

The Taj Mahal is famous for its stunning architecture, perfect symmetry, and detailed marble designs.
It combines Mughal, Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural styles with a large dome, four minarets, beautiful gardens, and artistic carvings.
It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
Millions of tourists visit it every year to admire its beauty and learn about its rich history.
The Taj Mahal remains a symbol of love and one of the greatest examples of human creativity.

📜 History

The history of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 AD during the reign of Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.
It was built as a symbol of love and remembrance for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, who passed away in 1631.
Shah Jahan ordered the construction of this magnificent monument in Agra, India, beside the Yamuna River.
Thousands of skilled workers, architects, and craftsmen worked for more than 20 years to complete the Taj Mahal.
The monument was constructed using white marble and decorated with precious stones and beautiful carvings.
The main architect was believed to be Ustad Ahmad Lahori, who designed this masterpiece with a perfect balance of beauty and symmetry.
After its completion around 1653 AD, the Taj Mahal became one of the greatest achievements of Mughal architecture and remains a symbol of love and history.

⭐ Why Famous

The Taj Mahal is famous because it is a symbol of eternal love built by Emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
It is known for its beautiful white marble architecture, perfect symmetry, and amazing artistic designs.
It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Millions of people visit the Taj Mahal every year to admire its beauty, history, and unique Mughal architecture.

🏗️ Architecture

The architecture of the Taj Mahal is a perfect combination of Mughal, Persian, Islamic, and Indian styles.
It is built with beautiful white marble and features a large central dome, four tall minarets, and a symmetrical design.
The monument is decorated with detailed carvings, floral patterns, and precious stone inlay work.
The surrounding gardens, fountains, and reflection pool add to its beauty and create a peaceful atmosphere.
The Taj Mahal's unique design and craftsmanship make it one of the greatest examples of world architecture.

✨ Interesting Facts

The Taj Mahal has many interesting facts that make it a unique monument.
It took around 22 years to complete, from 1632 AD to 1653 AD.
More than 20,000 workers, artists, and craftsmen helped build this masterpiece.
The Taj Mahal is made of white marble that changes color depending on the sunlight and time of day.
It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
The main dome of the Taj Mahal is about 73 meters high and is surrounded by four beautiful minarets.
The monument attracts millions of visitors from around the world every year.
